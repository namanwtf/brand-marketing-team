## Product Hunt Listing

### Tagline
Deploy a 24/7 AI Marketing Department in 60 seconds

### Description

Brand Marketing Team is the world's largest open-source AI marketing framework. It deploys 30 autonomous agents that handle every aspect of marketing—from competitive intelligence to viral growth.

**Not templates. Not prompts. Actual autonomous agents.**

### Key Features

🎯 **30 Specialized Agents**
Each masters one marketing discipline: competitive intel, pricing optimization, copywriting, Meta Ads, SEO, influencer outreach, video marketing, retention analysis, viral growth, and 20 more.

🤖 **Autonomous Operation**
Works 24/7 without manual intervention. Monitors competitors, optimizes pricing, writes copy, runs ads—all while you sleep.

⚡ **60-Second Setup**
Install, enter your website, and AI auto-discovers your brand. 30 marketing agents wake up immediately. No configuration needed.

📱 **Real-Time Alerts**
Get WhatsApp/Telegram notifications for competitor moves, price changes, and opportunities.

🌐 **Universal Framework**
Works for any brand, any industry, any market. SaaS, e-commerce, mobile apps, services—everything.

### How It Works

```bash
# Install
openclaw skills install https://github.com/namanwtf/brand-marketing-team

# Setup (60 seconds)
@auto-setup init
# Enter your website

# Done - 30 agents working 24/7
```
