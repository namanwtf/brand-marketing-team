# 📊 Analytics Dashboard

**Purpose:** Track competitive metrics, generate automated reports, and visualize market trends.

---

## Features

- **Price History Charts:** Track competitor pricing over time
- **Share of Voice:** Monitor brand mentions vs competitors
- **Automated Reports:** Daily, weekly, monthly PowerPoint exports
- **Trend Prediction:** ML-based price trend forecasting

---

## Commands

```bash
@analytics-dashboard generate daily
@analytics-dashboard competitor share-of-voice
@analytics-dashboard export ppt --weekly
@analytics-dashboard trend-forecast "next 30 days"
```

---

## Report Templates

### Daily Brief
- Competitor price changes
- New launches detected
- Stock availability alerts
- Recommended actions

### Weekly Report
- Price trend analysis
- Market share estimation
- Campaign performance
- Strategic recommendations

### Monthly Report
- Full competitive landscape
- Seasonal pattern analysis
- Pricing strategy review
- Next month forecast
